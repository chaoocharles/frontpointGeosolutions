SERVICES

WHAT WE DO BEST

GIS Development

With a growing demand for incorporation of GIS into businesses and companies to aid in the decision making process. FrontPoint Geosolutions prides itself in helping clients set up an efficient GIS System and GIS databases and equip them with the necessary skills and practices for data collection to help their organizations come up with helpful and economical solutions to real world and corporate problems such as route proximity analysis, business and market suitability locations, listing just but a few.

Remote Sensing

As a company we offer Earth Observation services through new and cutting edge technologies through Satellite and Aerial Image acquisition, Image processing and Analysis. Some of the sectors our services are utilized are in;

  • Natural Disaster Monitoring and Mitigation
  • Environmental monitoring
  • Forestry management
  • Agriculture and Food Security

Training Services

As a company that needs to integrate GIS into their internal framework in order to aid in decision making, FrontPoint Geosolutions offers the necessary resources to train your staff and equip them with the up-to-date GIS skills. This is made possible by our very qualified team of professional who have been trained and had extensive experience in the GIS and Survey field. Our services include;

  • Offering skills in latest technologies and software developments
  • Training in survey Instrument handling; GPS, Leveling, Total Station
  • Pre-processing and post-processing of field data collected for analysis
  • GIS Software training; ARCGIS, QGIS, ERDAS
  • GIS Programming through R and Python

Survey and Mapping

Front point Geosolutions combines current equipment, GIS software and professional staff members in offering a full suite of engineering survey, cadastral survey, topographic survey and hydrographic surveys depending on our clients ‘needs. We provide map outputs in the form of both static and interactive maps to aid in the visualization process.

  • Engineering survey
    • Setting out for engineering works i.e. Buildings, Roads and mines.
    • underground utilities setting out i.e. storm water drains, Sewers
    • Infrastructural and road construction survey works.

  • Topographic mapping
    • Accurate and detailed picking of features for architectural and construction design.
    • Incorporation of aerial photography and high resolution satellite imagery is used to improve the outputs generated.

  • Cadastral survey
    • Land subdivision and amalgamation of land parcels.
    • Mutation surveys.
    • Beacon establishment and re-establishment.
    • Deed plan and Title Deed processing
    • We advise our clients on the status of land ownership and rights, land restrictions and recording the information to help them develop different plans.
    • Land capability assessments and general land use planning for government agencies and different institutions.

  • Drone Mapping
    • Use of survey Drones to create high resolution orthomosaics in creation of aerial profiles.
    • Creation of 3D models of accessible and inaccessible areas.
